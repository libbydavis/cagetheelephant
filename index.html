About Cage The Elephant

American alt rock group, Cage The Elephant, formed in 2006 currently has 6 members: Matt Schultz (lead vocals), Brad Shultz (rhythm guitarist), Nick Bockrath (lead guitarist), Matthan Minster (guitatist and keyboardist), Daniel Tichenor (bassist) and Jared Champion (drummer). Lincoln Parish (former lead guitarist) was in the band until 2013 but left to pursue producing). Their name came about after a show when a mentally ill man approached the band and repeated ‘You have to cage the elephant’.
